MBTI Test

McDonald's Burger Type Indicator



Lettuce Be Honest

Someone who loves a lot of lettuce in their burger is probably the kind of person who wants it all — the crunch, the freshness, and that satisfying “stacked” feeling. They’re a little extra in the best way, turning a simple burger into a mini salad party. It shows they like balance: “Yes, I want a juicy burger, but I also want it to feel fresh and alive.” They’re playful, maybe a little dramatic (their burger is taller than everyone else’s), and they’re not afraid to admit that even the “sidekick ingredient” deserves the spotlight sometimes.
